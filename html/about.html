Andre Nurse is a former dancer andgymnast who competed on the Men’s Junior Canadian National Team in 2001and Placed 4th overall at the Canadian National Championships.

Andre was also a Power Tumbler champion placing 1st inthe Novice and Junior National categories at the Canadian National Championships in 1996 and 1997. In 1998 Andre placed 8th at the WorldPower Tumbling Championships in Sydney, Australia.In 2003 Andre used his Gymnastic skills and ran away to join the circus, notjust an ordinary circus but Cirque du Soleil in Las Vegas. Over the next 5 years Andre zoned into a newly found talent using acrobatics and theatrewhich transformed him , along with fellow performers and audience membersinto another world twice daily, five days a week. While at “Mystere" at theTreasure Island Hotel in Las Vegas, Andre performed on the bungee trapeze,Chinese poles as well, tumbling, dancing, stilt walking, rappelling, and taiko drumming.

Andre is still actively training and preforming while transitioning to a behindthe scenes creative position. In 2016 he was granted the opportunity to doaerial choreography and co- produce a show with “TAG” Talent ArtisticLimited and “Livewire Productions” for Celebrity Cruises Productions thatwill be touring the Caribbean and Mediterranean until 2020. In 2014 Andreand Amanda co-write, co- produced, directed and performed in “Gym Giantand the Genie”, to help support the grand opening of Gymnastics Giants theirformer place of employment.
