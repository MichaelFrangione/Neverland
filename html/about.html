About Neverland Arts & Entertainment

Neverland Arts & Entertainments Inc. was registered as a Corporation in February 2015. We are scheduled to open for full operation in 2016 as a multi complex sports/arts facility. Neverland is designed to cater to a clientele of all ages.

Our Mission: Helping people in the Peel Region reach their highest athletic and performance goals in their respective disciplines as an athlete or performer with the highest quality in coaching, training, and management methods.

Neverland will be a one of a kind “one stop shop” facility with an athletic and production component to our business structure. We will focus on the athletic side of the business by helping clients/members from toddler to adult realize their athletic potential in a fun, safe, hardworking environment.

From the production side of the business, athletes have the opportunity to graduate from a competitive career into a performance career or a behind the scenes position as part of the creative team or technical team, for future shows employed by Neverland Productions. Not many, if any sporting facilities can claim that they offer such growth potential to its clientele.

At Neverland we offer a plethora of activities and classes for kids and adults, including Gymnastics, Power Tumbling, Cheerleading, Circus Arts, Skateboarding, Trampoline, Rock Climbing(coming soon), Dance (coming soon) since they complement each other. At Neverland we believe that if you can dream it, it can be reality! So our goal at Neverland is to blur the line between dreams and reality. We like to call it Dreamality! At Neverland we’re always open to new ideas and strongly encourage collaboration, it takes a village, to do stuff, or something like that (lol). Let us help each other turn our dreams into reality.

Meet the Team

Director of Finance & Human Resources

Deon Clements

Deon Clements is a double major recipient who graduated from Southern Illinois University receiving her Bachelors in Finance and Economic and entered into the CGA Designated program in Ontario, Canada after her graduation in 1993. Deon has over 18 years’ Canadian experience in management, managing within Accounting and Finance Departments with a team as small as 3 people and as large as 36 people. The companies operating staffing sizes ranged from 350 to 7000 people with annual revenue from 500M to 900B annually with international and multi currencies operations.

Deon is bringing experiences from the private and public sections, working with organizations in construction, the service sector, manufacturing and currently consumer distribution sales. Some of Deon’s management experiences includes financial statements preparation and presentations, cashflow preparations, system implementations, workflow solutions, financial analysis, employees reviews, hiring and termination, Standard Operating Practice and manual writing, streamlining and system automation, audit involvements (governments, tax recovery, due diligent and year-end close).

Deon is a resident of Brampton where she lives with her husband and 10 year old daughter.

Director of Production

Andre Nurse

Andre Nurse is a former dancer and gymnast who competed on the Men’s Junior Canadian National Team in 2001 and Placed 4th overall at the Canadian National Championships.

Andre was also a Power Tumbler champion placing 1st in the Novice and Junior National categories at the Canadian National Championships in 1996 and 1997. In 1998 Andre placed 8th at the World Power Tumbling Championships in Sydney, Australia. In 2003 Andre used his Gymnastic skills and ran away to join the circus, not just an ordinary circus but Cirque du Soleil in Las Vegas. Over the next 5 years Andre zoned into a newly found talent using acrobatics and theatre which transformed him, along with fellow performers and audience members into another world twice daily, five days a week. While at “Mystere" at the Treasure Island Hotel in Las Vegas, Andre performed on the bungee trapeze, Chinese poles as well, tumbling, dancing, stilt walking, rappelling, and taiko drumming.

Andre is still actively training and preforming while transitioning to a behind the scenes creative position. In 2016 he was granted the opportunity to do aerial choreography and co-produce a show with “TAG” Talent Artistic Limited and “Livewire Productions” for Celebrity Cruises Productions that will be touring the Caribbean and Mediterranean until 2020. In 2014 Andre and Amanda co-write, co-produced, directed and performed in “Gym Giant and the Genie”, to help support the grand opening of Gymnastics Giants their former place of employment.
