Recreational Gymnastics

Beginner Boys/Girls (4-7yrs)

*(boys and girls together)

This class is an introduction into gymnastics for your child. Parent and tot are led by a certified coach, teaching running, jumping, bouncing, swinging balance and much more. Students will improve gross motor skills as well as self trust and cooperation in a safe and fun environment.

Beginner Development Program (8-13yrs)

*(boys and girls separate)

This is an introduction into the development program where athletes will learn to be confident in their abilities in the gym as well as in all areas of life. Athletes will become more independent as their skills progress.

Showcase Boys/Girls (5-8yrs/8-13yrs)

*(boys and girls separate)

Students will be practicing the basics and learning new more advanced skills in this class. The students will learn how to set goals for themselves and work hard to achieve them all while making new friends and building their self esteem.

Tricking/Parkour

Tricking a training discipline that combines martial arts kicks with flips and twists from gymnastics as well as many dance moves and styles from breakdancing. Parkour involves seeing one's environment in a new way, and imagining the potentialities for navigating it by movement around, across, through, over and under its features using only the human body. This class will help in learning these tricks in a safe environment using mats and blocks.
