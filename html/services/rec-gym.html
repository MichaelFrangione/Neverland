Tricking/Parkour

(Price - $105.00/mth - $170.00/mth)

Tricking a training discipline that combines martial arts kicks with flips and twists from gymnastics as well as many dance moves and styles from breakdancing. Parkour involves seeing one's environment in a new way, and imagining the potentialities for navigating it by movement around, across, though, over and under its features using only the human body. This class will help in learning these tricks in a safe environment using mats and blocks.
