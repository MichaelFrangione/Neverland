Kinder Gym

Parent n Tot (18mth - 2 1/2)

(Price - $80.00/mth - $110.00/mth)

This class is an introduction into gymnas-tics for your child. Parent and tot are led by a certified coach, teaching running, jumping, bouncing, swinging balance and much more. Students will improve gross motor skills as well as self-trust and cooperation in a safe and fun environment.

Kinder (2-4 yrs.)

(Price - $80.00/mth - $110.00/mth)

This class's focus is on fun, fitness and fundamentals of gymnastics. Students will be led by a certified coach in learning the basics of gymnastics. They will learn all the basic shape jumps, front rolls, back rolls, hanging and swinging on the bars. They will improve their balance on the beam and learn how to jump on the trampoline. This class will build and develop independence.
