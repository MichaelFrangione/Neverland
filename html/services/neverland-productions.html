Chinese Pole

Chinese Pole takes ground acrobatic skills to new heights! Whether holding difficult static positions or kicking off the pole to perform somersaults, heart skipping drops or slides, the Chinese Pole will keep the audience at the edge of their seats. As an aerial discipline, it's interesting. Different to Corde Lisse or silks or trapeze because the terrain of the equipment is always unyielding. The pole is rigid, and there's no way to wrap-in or to truly rest; it's simultaneously easier to climb and harder to be on, requiring precise skill and accuracy.

Aerial Silks

Aerial Silks is a beautiful form of dance and acrobatic skills performed within the aerial fabric. This high flying performance blends intricate wraps, graceful swings and spins together with gravity defying drops that will leave audiences amazed. Flexibility, strength and artistry combined with grace and elegance will create an exhilarating atmosphere for any occasion.

Partner Acrobatic Dance

This routine encompasses elements of gymnastics, tumbling, acrobatic lifts, turns and dance set to music that will leave audiences begging for more. This ground routine combines strength, flexibility, and balance, along with dynamic throws, somersaults, twists and catches, making it suitable for any function.
