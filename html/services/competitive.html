Inter club Boys/Girls

This is an advanced class for gymnasts who are looking for a little more of a challenge. Students must have the prerequisite skills to join. This class will help students to build their skills and physical fitness as well as teach them commitment and team bonding. Student are encouraged to overcome fears and grow as athletes.

Power Tumbling (7+years)

This discipline combines artistic gymnastics and trampoline acrobatics performed on a 25m long track. This class will teach students basic to advanced tumbling acrobatics in a fun environment.

Acro Sport

A discipline of gymnastics encompassing elements of gymnastics, tumbling, acrobatic lifts, turns and dance set to music. In this class students will be working with others to perform skills in partners, trios or quads. They will be learning to develop their communication, musicality, and team work, while having fun and making friends.
